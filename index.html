#whats-this

The aim of the Discord is to create nusociety. I'm not entirely sure what nusociety should or will be, but it's based around this idea:
>>>Humans work best when they're in small communities where each person has purpose<<<
The plan for the near future is to gather a significant amount of active members, each with a role/purpose that he/she is happy with. and eventually then split into smaller groups with no communication between groups aside from group leaders.
The smaller groups will then agree on a purpose and work together towards this purpose, it could be anything eg. Art project, album, music video, charity awareness, whatever that group wants.
The groups will engage in healthy competition to encourage projects to be successful. The most successful projects will get the most exposure to the whole society.
No one is expected to do anything to be a part of this, if you just want to chat and be along for the ride thats absolutely fine. But Ideally you should find a role within a group. Even if your role is something as simple as entertaining everyone.
